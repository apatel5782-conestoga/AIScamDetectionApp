## Overall Reflection

Using AI tools significantly improved our productivity during the capstone project. AI helped generate ideas faster, solve coding issues, improve design quality, and prepare professional documentation. Without AI, debugging and writing structured documentation would have taken much longer. However, our team reviewed, tested, and customized all AI-generated suggestions ourselves. AI supported our learning process but did not replace our understanding or teamwork.
